Business Snapshot

Garage epoxy flooring transforms plain concrete garage floors into durable, attractive, and easy-to-clean surfaces using specialized polyurethane or epoxy coatings.

This home service appeals to homeowners who want their garage to look like an extension of their living space rather than a dull, dusty storage area.

Primarily a B2C model, this business targets middle-to-upper income homeowners aged 35‚Äì65, with occasional work for small commercial spaces such as auto shops and warehouses. The results are visually striking, easy to market, and in high demand during warmer months.

Final Notes

This business offers strong margins, a simple model, and high perceived value. The lack of recurring revenue is a drawback, but strong referrals and a short sales cycle keep the pipeline moving.

Nik's Verdict

A proven, high-margin trade service with low complexity and strong homeowner appeal.

Seasonal dips are manageable with a solid lead generation system.

Bottom Line

If you don't mind hands-on work and can manage the seasonality, garage epoxy flooring can be a consistent and profitable business.
